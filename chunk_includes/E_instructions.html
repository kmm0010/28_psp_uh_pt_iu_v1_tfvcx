Instructions

You will hear a sequence of sounds that will sound similar to an English word.
(But, it won't be a real word.)

You are to pay attention to the last consonant sound in the first syllable.

These fake words will sound like either spup-ee or sput-ee and like either bup-ee or but-ee.

You are deciding whether you heard T or P at then end of the first syllable.

For example, if you hear ‘spup-ee’, then you will press the P button.
And, if you hear ‘bup-ee’, then you will also press the P button.
If you hear ‘sput-ee’, then you will press the T button.
And, if you hear ‘but-ee’, then you will press the T button.

Press any key to continue.
