Instructions

You will hear a sequence of sounds that will sound similar to an English word.
(But, it won't be a real word.)

You are to pay attention to the last consonant sound in the first syllable and the last vowel.
In other words, the last two sounds.

These fake words will sound like one of following spup-ee, sput-ee, spup-oo, sput-oo, bup-ee, but-ee, bup-oo, or but-oo.

You are deciding whether you heard T or P at then end of the first syllable and whether you heard EE or OO for the final vowel.

For example, if you hear ‘sput-ee’, then you will press the T–EE button.
And, if you hear ‘but-ee’, then you will also press the T–EE button.
But, if you hear ‘but-oo’, then you will press the T–OO button.
Or, if you hear ‘bup-ee’, then you will press the P–EE button.

Press any key to continue.
